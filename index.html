One Verse. Every Campus.

A simple project sharing Scripture across college campuses.

Each day, one Bible verse is designed to be easily shared and reposted by students and campus ministries across the country.

Our goal is to spread the Gospel to ALL college students through verses and encouragement — not just those involved in campus ministries. This is done through college ministries across the nation reposting our daily verses on Instagram's shared school story for their particular campus.
